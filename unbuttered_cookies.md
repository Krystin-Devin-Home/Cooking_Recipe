## unbuttered_cookies
- Healthy snacks!
- Total time: 30 min

### Ingredience
- 1 cup of all purpose flour
- 1/3 cup of brown sugar 
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp baking powder

### Steps
- Mix all the stuff together and beat until soft
- Scoop mixture into a pan with baking paper on top
- Bake for 15min
